Welcome to Solar!

Who are we?

We are Solar, A group of three college students who want to give back
to other college students. We have developed Solar as a flash card application
to help other students study. Our motto is "Reach for the stars!"

What is our goal?

Our goal at Solar is to help other students, regardless of what level of
education they are in, to study efficiently and quickly. Using flashcards have
been scientifically shown to increase one's memory!

Get Started!

To get started with Solar, begin by looking near the top of the page.
There are a few buttons where you are able to create new flashcards,
create a new flashcard Set, and even edit some basic account info!
